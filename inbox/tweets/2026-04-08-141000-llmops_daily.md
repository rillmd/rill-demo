The three most undervalued LLM evals nobody runs until it hurts:

1. Hallucination rate on grounded tasks
2. Latency tail (p99, not mean)
3. Cost per successful completion

Accuracy on a golden set is the easy one. These three are what actually blow up your roadmap in production.
