Weekly page synthesizing major technical insights and decisions. Generated from new knowledge notes tagged as insights or references. Should capture the week's major themes and show connections between them.

Source hints:
- `knowledge/notes/` entries from the past 7 days with `type: insight` or `type: reference`
- Completed tasks from the past week
- Workspace session history entries from the past week

Structure is not prescribed — group by theme, not by day. Prefer 3-4 major themes over a long list. Always include a "Connection Points" section that ties the themes together; that's where the real value is. End with a forward-looking "Next Week" section.
