The first async standup was surprisingly effective. A few observations from day one that I want to hold onto:

- Jordan's written update had noticeably more depth than his typical verbal standup. He surfaced a subtle issue with the retry-after header parsing that would have been a two-sentence throwaway in a live meeting but became a concrete action item in writing. Worth calling out.
