# Daily Note -- April 6, 2026

## Today's Focus

Day one of the async standup experiment. The team agreed on Friday to a two-week trial, and today is the first real test. The format is simple: each person posts a written update to the team channel by 10am covering yesterday's progress, today's plan, and any blockers. No live meeting. I want to pay close attention to how the information density compares with the verbal standup -- my hypothesis is that the forcing function of writing will actually produce better updates, but it only holds if people commit to the format.

The second priority is making real progress on the typed error schema spec. Last week I had a rough draft; today's goal is a v0.5 that's concrete enough to review in a PR. Specifically, nailing down the error category taxonomy (I'm leaning toward five categories: validation, authentication, authorization, resource, and system) and defining the retryable semantics precisely.
