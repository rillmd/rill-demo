# Async Standup Proposal Draft

## Goal

Draft the async standup format proposal for team review, run a trial, and decide whether to adopt it permanently.

## Background

Daily live standups were eating 15-20 minutes every morning and rarely surfaced anything that couldn't have been written down. With the team split across time zones (Alex in PT, Jordan on late hours this month), two or three people were always half-awake. An async format — written updates by 10am local, with an optional sync if something needs discussion — seemed worth trying.

## Context

The proposal had three parts: the format itself (what goes in an update), the trigger rules (when do we fall back to a live meeting), and the trial period (one week starting April 6).

## History

- 2026-04-03: Task created from April 1 journal entry where the frustration with live standups came up
- 2026-04-04: First draft written, shared with Alex and Jordan for feedback
- 2026-04-06: Team approved the proposal, trial started same day
- 2026-04-10: Trial week complete. Consensus: keep the async format. Marking task done.
